RiseUp Connect

Over the years RiseUp managed to connect stakeholders to local & global networks, also fusing diverse talents and resources to fulfill startups needs for the most relevant resources from experts. RiseUpConnect aims to build a healthy environment for all our stakeholders and especially Startups. RiseUpConnect aims to help startups whether they are in the early stage or in the developed stage. We provide many features and the two most important features are the Startup Kit and Office Hours.

Perks

Connect enables startups to get access to exclusive offerings of tools and services that are tailored based on their needs at different stages.
